Types of

HPV Vaccines

HPV vaccines offer different levels of protection. Let us guide
you through the best options for your health and safety.

CERVARIX

Cervarix targets HPV types 16 and 18, which cause about 70% of cervical cancer cases. It's designed specifically
prevent cervical cancer and works best when given before HPV exposure, typically between ages 9-14.
Cervarix also offers some protection against other high-risk HPV types like 31 and 45. It doesn't treat existing
infections but helps prevent future HPV-related cervical diseases and complications.

The vaccines is given in three doses over six months (0, 1 and 6 months) and is widely approved as a safe
effective preventive health measure.
